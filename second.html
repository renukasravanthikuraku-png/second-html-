POWER STAR PAVAN KALYAN

NAME:Konidela Pawan Kalyan[5]
BORN: Konidela Sri Kalyan Kumar;
DATE OF BIRTH: 2 September 1971
POLICY: Indian politician and actor, serving as the 11th Deputy Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh since June 2024.
He is also the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development and Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forest, Science and Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh as MLA representing the Pithapuram constituency.
He is the founder and president of the Janasena Party.
